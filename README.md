# Forecasting India monthly merchandise export
This project presents a comprehensive analysis of India's monthly merchandise export data spanning January 2018 to December 2025, covering 251 destination countries and
96 monthly observations totaling approximately $3.086 trillion in aggregate exports.
The study achieves three objectives  (1) Building and comparing two classes of forecasting models ARIMAX, LSTM  
(2) constructing a country-level volatility matrix based on  quantitative metrics and 
(3) analyzing the correlations between export performance and macroeconomic indicators GDP growth rate, consumer price in ation, and Real Effective Exchange Rate (REER)
